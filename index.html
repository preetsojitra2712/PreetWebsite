About Me

Hi, I'm Preet Sojitra — a Machine Learning Engineer and Software Developer passionate about building intelligent, scalable systems that bridge AI research with real-world applications. Beyond that, I've built projects ranging from a real-time credit card fraud detection platform with Kafka, Spark, and Prometheus, to MedQuery, a hybrid medical search engine combining Lucene BM25 and BERT-based semantic retrieval.

I love working at the intersection of AI, data systems, and product engineering — where thoughtful design meets high-performance code. Whether it's integrating large language models into production workflows or optimizing cloud infrastructure for intelligent applications, I'm driven by curiosity and impact.

My goal is to help design the next generation of AI-driven products that are not only powerful, but also ethical, scalable, and human-centered.

Achieved mAP: 87.0% / Rank-1: 90.8% on MARS and mAP: 97.1% on DukeMTMC — a +4.9% mAP gain over baselines.
